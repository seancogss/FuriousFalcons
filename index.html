Who Are We?

Team 4328 is a robotics team from Foster High School in Richmond, Texas, with 30 high school student members. As an Open Alliance team, we share our CAD and code with the world, believing that true innovation happens through transparency.

As a non-profit organization running a high-budget program, we rely on sponsor donations, both in-kind and cash, to continue the work we do. Our program inspires young minds through mentor-based robotics, fostering the skills needed to solve the world's most complex challenges.
